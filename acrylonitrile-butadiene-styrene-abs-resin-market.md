<p><strong>The Acrylonitrile-Butadiene-Styrene (ABS) Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emulsion Graft Copolymerization</li><li>Bulk Copolymerization</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ABS resin market can be categorized into three types: emulsion graft copolymerization, bulk copolymerization, and others. In emulsion graft copolymerization, the process involves copolymerizing acrylonitrile, butadiene, and styrene in the presence of a stabilizing agent. This method allows for fine particle size and better heat resistance. In bulk copolymerization, ABS resin is produced through the polymerization of the three monomers in a single liquid phase. This method offers superior transparency and impact strength. The "others" category includes alternative methods and technologies used in ABS resin production.</p></p>
