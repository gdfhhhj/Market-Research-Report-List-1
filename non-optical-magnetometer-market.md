<p><strong>The Non-Optical Magnetometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Magnetometer</li><li>Relative Magnetometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-optical magnetometer market can be divided into two main types: absolute magnetometers and relative magnetometers. </p><p>Absolute magnetometers measure the actual magnetic field strength and direction at a particular location. These instruments are highly accurate and used in applications such as geophysics, navigation, and aerospace.</p><p>On the other hand, relative magnetometers measure changes in magnetic field strength, rather than providing absolute values. They are commonly used in areas like mineral exploration, transportation, and consumer electronics to detect variations in magnetic fields.</p><p>Both types play a significant role in various industries, each with its specific advantages and applications.</p></p>
