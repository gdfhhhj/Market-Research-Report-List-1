<p><p>The nanomembrane market is segmented into three types: polymeric membrane, hybrid membrane, and inorganic membrane. Polymeric membranes are made from synthetic polymers, offering excellent flexibility and durability. Hybrid membranes combine the benefits of both organic and inorganic materials, providing enhanced selectivity and stability. Inorganic membranes are composed of inorganic materials such as ceramics or metals, offering superior thermal and chemical resistance. Each membrane type has its unique advantages, making them suitable for various applications in industries such as water treatment, gas separation, and biomedical research.</p></p>
