<p><p>The waterproof surgical tapes market comprises two major types: PE and Non-Woven Fabric. PE tapes are made from polyethylene, a durable and waterproof material that provides excellent adhesion and protection. On the other hand, non-woven fabric tapes are composed of synthetic fibers that are bonded together, offering high breathability, tear resistance, and water repellency. Both types of tapes are commonly used in medical procedures where maintaining a dry wound site or securing medical equipment in wet or humid environments is crucial.</p></p>
