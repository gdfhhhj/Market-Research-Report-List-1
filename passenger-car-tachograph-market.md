<p><p>The passenger car tachograph market is divided into two types: integrated and portable. Integrated tachographs are built-in systems within the vehicle's dashboard and are permanently installed. They provide continuous monitoring of various vehicle parameters and driver activities. On the other hand, portable tachographs are compact devices that can be easily installed or removed from the vehicle. They offer similar functionality as integrated tachographs but provide flexibility for use in different vehicles or for temporary monitoring purposes. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring compliance with regulations and improving safety in the passenger car industry.</p></p>
