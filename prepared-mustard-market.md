<p><p>Prepared mustard is available in two main market types: organic and conventional. Organic mustard is made from mustard seeds that are grown without the use of synthetic fertilizers, pesticides, or genetically modified organisms (GMOs). It follows strict organic regulations for cultivation and processing. On the other hand, conventional mustard is produced using conventionally-grown mustard seeds that may involve the use of synthetic inputs. While both types offer the distinctive tangy flavor and versatility of mustard, organic mustard appeals to those seeking a product that is environmentally friendly, free from artificial additives, and supports sustainable farming practices.</p></p>
