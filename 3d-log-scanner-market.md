<p><strong>The 3D Log Scanner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser</li><li>X-Ray</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D Log Scanner market encompasses two primary types: Laser and X-Ray scanners. Laser scanners employ laser beams to capture detailed information about the dimensions, shape, and surface texture of logs, allowing for precise measurements and analysis necessary for various industries like forestry and wood processing. On the other hand, X-Ray scanners utilize X-ray technology to capture internal features of logs, such as knots, defects, and density variations. This information aids in quality control, grading, and decision-making processes for log processing companies. Both types play an essential role in improving efficiency and accuracy in the log scanning industry.</p></p>
