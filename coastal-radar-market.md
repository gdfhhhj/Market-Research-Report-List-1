<p><p>Coastal radar is a type of radar system used specifically for monitoring and detecting activities in coastal regions. The market for coastal radar can be categorized into three types: X-Band radar, S-Band radar, and other radar systems. X-Band radar operates at a higher frequency range, providing high-resolution images and accurate target detection. S-Band radar operates at a lower frequency range, allowing for longer detection range and better penetration through atmospheric conditions. The "other" category includes radar systems that may operate at different frequency ranges or utilize alternative technologies for specific coastal surveillance requirements.</p></p>
