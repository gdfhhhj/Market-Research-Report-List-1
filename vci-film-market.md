<p><p>VCI Film, or Vapor Corrosion Inhibitor Film, is a packaging material specifically designed to protect metal parts and machinery from corrosion during storage or transport. VCI Stretch Film is a flexible and elastic film that is used to wrap around large or irregularly shaped objects to provide a tight and secure barrier against moisture and corrosion. VCI Shrink Film, on the other hand, is a heat-shrinkable film that, when heated, shrinks tightly around the object's contours, ensuring a snug and corrosion-resistant fit.
