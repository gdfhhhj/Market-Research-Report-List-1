The second type has a range of XYZ direction ±12±35±75mm, meaning it can measure movements and deformations in three directions (X, Y, and Z) with different maximum limits: ±12mm for X direction, ±35mm for Y direction, and ±75mm for Z direction. These jointmeters are essential tools for studying the behavior and stability of rock and soil structures.</p></p>
