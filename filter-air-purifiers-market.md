<p><p>Filter air purifiers are categorized into three types: stand-alone filter air purifiers, in-duct filter air purifiers, and others. Stand-alone filter air purifiers are independent devices that can be placed in any room for air purification purposes. In-duct filter air purifiers are installed within the HVAC system, effectively purifying the air throughout the entire building. The "others" category includes portable filter air purifiers that can be worn or carried, as well as larger units used in industrial settings. These different types cater to various needs for clean air in different environments.</p></p>
