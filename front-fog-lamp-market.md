<p><p>The front fog lamp market is comprised of three main types: LED, Xenon, and Halogen. LED fog lamps utilize light-emitting diodes to generate bright and clear illumination while consuming less energy and having a longer lifespan. Xenon fog lamps, also known as HID (High-Intensity Discharge) lamps, produce a intense and white-colored light by igniting xenon gas, ensuring enhanced visibility. On the other hand, Halogen fog lamps employ a tungsten filament that emits a bright white light and has a relatively lower cost compared to LED and Xenon options.</p></p>
