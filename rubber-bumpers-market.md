Neoprene rubber bumpers are resistant to oil, heat, and weathering, making them suitable for outdoor and automotive applications. EPDM rubber bumpers offer excellent resistance to ozone, UV radiation, and aging, making them ideal for outdoor and harsh environmental conditions. Nitrile rubber bumpers are oil-resistant and find applications in the automotive and oil industry. The "Others" category includes rubber bumpers made from different materials to meet specific requirements.</p></p>
