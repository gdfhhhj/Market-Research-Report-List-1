<p><strong>The Subsea Pressure Transmitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Differential Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Gauge Pressure Transmitters</li><li>Multivariable Transmitters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The subsea pressure transmitter market is divided into four types. Absolute pressure transmitters measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum. Differential pressure transmitters measure the difference in pressure between two points. Gauge pressure transmitters measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure. Multivariable transmitters measure multiple process variables simultaneously, such as pressure, temperature, and flow rate. These different types of transmitters are used in different subsea applications to accurately measure pressure and provide essential data for various operations.</p></p>
