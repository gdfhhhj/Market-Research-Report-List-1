<p><p>PP (polypropylene) compounds are thermoplastic materials made by blending PP resin with various additives to enhance its properties. The market for PP compounds is segmented into homopolymer type and copolymer type. Homopolymer PP compounds are made solely from PP resin and have high stiffness, excellent chemical resistance, and good heat resistance, making them suitable for applications such as automotive components and appliances. Copolymer PP compounds, on the other hand, are made by blending PP resin with other polymers, resulting in improved impact resistance, flexibility, and transparency. They find applications in packaging, consumer goods, and medical devices.</p></p>
