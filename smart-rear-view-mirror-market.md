<p><p>The Smart Rear View Mirror market consists of two types: Body Mounted and Door Mounted. Body Mounted mirrors are directly attached to the body of the vehicle, providing a clear rear view for the driver. This type offers a wider field of view and eliminates blind spots. On the other hand, Door Mounted mirrors are installed on the doors, usually near the driver's side window. These mirrors offer a narrower field of view but can be easily adjusted by the driver to improve visibility.</p></p>
