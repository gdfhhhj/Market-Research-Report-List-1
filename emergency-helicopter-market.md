<p><p>The Emergency Helicopter market consists of single-rotor and double-rotor helicopters. Single-rotor helicopters, also known as mono-rotor or rotorcraft, have one main rotor providing vertical lift and a tail rotor for stability. They offer greater agility and maneuverability, making them suitable for various emergency operations such as search and rescue missions. On the other hand, double-rotor helicopters, also called tandem-rotor or dual-rotor helicopters, have two main rotors placed one in front of the other. This configuration allows for increased lifting capacity and stability, making them ideal for transporting larger loads during emergency situations.</p></p>
