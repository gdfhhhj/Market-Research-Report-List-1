<p><p>The automotive cables market comprises two types: copper core and aluminum core. Copper core cables are made of copper conductors, which offer excellent electrical conductivity. These cables are preferred for their low resistance, high tensile strength, and durability. On the other hand, aluminum core cables use aluminum conductors, which are lighter in weight and more cost-effective. However, they have relatively higher resistance and lower tensile strength compared to copper core cables. The choice between copper and aluminum core cables depends on the specific automotive application, considering factors like cost, performance requirements, and weight constraints.</p></p>
