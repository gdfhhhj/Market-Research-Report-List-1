<p><p>The industrial processing pectinase market includes two types of enzymes: acid pectinase and alkaline pectinase. Acid pectinase is used for industrial processes that require low pH conditions, such as fruit juice production. It breaks down pectin, a complex carbohydrate found in fruits, into simpler components. Alkaline pectinase, on the other hand, is used in industrial processes that require high pH conditions, like textile and paper industries. It also breaks down pectin but is effective in alkaline environments. Both types of pectinase enzymes play crucial roles in various industrial processes.</p></p>
