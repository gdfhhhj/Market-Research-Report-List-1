<p><p>The automotive multi-zone air conditioning system market includes various types such as 2-zone, 3-zone, 4-zone, and others. A 2-zone system allows separate temperature control for the driver and front passenger, ensuring personalized comfort. Similarly, a 3-zone system extends this capability to the rear seat passengers, enabling them to control their temperature settings individually. A 4-zone system further adds the capacity for separate temperature control for the rear-left and rear-right passengers. The "others" category refers to systems that may offer additional zones or unique features beyond the standard configurations.</p></p>
