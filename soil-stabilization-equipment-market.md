<p><p>Soil stabilization equipment market can be classified into various types based on their functions and applications. Reclaimers are used to pulverize and mix the existing pavement material to create a stabilized base. Mixers are used to blend different materials, such as cement or lime, with soil to improve its strength and stability. Rotors are rotating drums used for mixing and distributing binder materials into the soil. Spreaders are used to evenly distribute the mixed materials on the ground. Other types of soil stabilization equipment include crushers, compactors, graders, and pavers.</p></p>
