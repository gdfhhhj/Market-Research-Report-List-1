<p><p>The 3-axis magnetometers market comprises two types: digital type and analog type. Digital magnetometers provide digital output in the form of binary data, making them suitable for use in digital systems like smartphones and computers. They offer high accuracy, stability, and noise immunity. Analog magnetometers, on the other hand, provide analog output signals proportional to the magnetic field's intensity. They are commonly used in industrial applications where real-time measurements are required. Both types cater to different market segments based on their distinct features and applications.</p></p>
