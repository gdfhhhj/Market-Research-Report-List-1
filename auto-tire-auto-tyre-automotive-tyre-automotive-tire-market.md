<p><p>The auto tire market comprises two main types: bias tire and radial tire. Bias tires are made with layers of fabric crisscrossing each other at a specific angle, providing high load-carrying capacity but less fuel efficiency and a rougher ride. On the other hand, radial tires are constructed with layers of cords running perpendicular to the tire center, resulting in improved fuel efficiency, smoother ride, and better traction. These types differ in their manufacturing process and performance characteristics, catering to different consumer preferences and driving conditions.</p></p>
