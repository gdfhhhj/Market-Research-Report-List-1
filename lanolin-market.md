<p><p>Lanolin is a greasy substance obtained from sheep's wool. Two types of lanolin markets exist - anhydrous lanolin and hydrous lanolin. Anhydrous lanolin is obtained by removing the water content from the raw lanolin, resulting in a semi-solid waxy product. It is commonly used in skincare products and cosmetics due to its excellent moisturizing properties. On the other hand, hydrous lanolin retains some water content and is often used in various pharmaceutical, industrial, and personal care applications. Both types of lanolin cater to different industries and have their unique uses.</p></p>
