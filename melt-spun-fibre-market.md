<p><p>The Melt Spun Fibre Market consists of two main types: Filament Melt-Spun Fibers and Staple Melt-Spun Fibers. Filament Melt-Spun Fibers are continuous strands of fiber that are manufactured by melting polymer chips and then extruding them through spinnerets. These fibers have high tensile strength and are commonly used in applications such as textiles, ropes, and fishing lines. On the other hand, Staple Melt-Spun Fibers are shorter lengths of fibers that are typically cut during the manufacturing process. They are used in various industries including nonwoven fabrics, automotive, and geotextiles.</p></p>
