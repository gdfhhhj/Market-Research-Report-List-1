<p><p>The Lightweight Coated Paper (LWC) market comprises various types such as Matt LWC Paper, Gloss LWC Paper, and Silk LWC Paper. Matt LWC Paper has a non-reflective, matte finish, making it suitable for applications like magazine pages, brochures, and catalogs. Gloss LWC Paper is characterized by a shiny, reflective surface that enhances visual appeal, often used in high-quality magazines and promotional materials. Silk LWC Paper lies between matt and gloss, offering a smooth, slightly shiny finish suitable for a wide range of printing needs, including magazines, flyers, and inserts.</p></p>
