<p><p>The metallic industrial gasket market comprises various types of gaskets, including spiral wound, ring joint, corrugated, and others. Spiral wound gaskets are made of metallic and non-metallic materials and can withstand high pressures. Ring joint gaskets are designed for flanges and provide a pressure seal in high-pressure and high-temperature applications. Corrugated gaskets have a corrugated profile that allows for compression and flexibility. Other types of gaskets include serrated, camprofile, and double-jacketed gaskets, each offering unique properties for specific industrial purposes.</p></p>
