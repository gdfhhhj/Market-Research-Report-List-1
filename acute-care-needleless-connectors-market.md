<p><p>The Acute Care Needleless Connectors Market comprises three types: Positive Displacement, Negative Displacement, and Neutral Displacement. Positive Displacement connectors have a built-in mechanism that ensures fluid flow when connected, minimizing the risk of blood reflux. Negative Displacement connectors rely on the clinician to manually withdraw and discard the required amount of fluid before accessing the line to prevent blood reflux. Neutral Displacement connectors incorporate a passive mechanism that prevents reflux without requiring additional steps. Each type offers unique benefits, and healthcare professionals choose the appropriate connector based on patient needs and clinical preferences.</p></p>
