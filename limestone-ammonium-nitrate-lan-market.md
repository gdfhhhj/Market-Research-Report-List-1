<p><p>Limestone Ammonium Nitrate (LAN) is a nitrogen-based fertilizer that consists of limestone (calcium carbonate) and ammonium nitrate. It is widely used in agriculture as a source of nitrogen, as it provides a slow-release form of this essential nutrient for plant growth. The limestone component in LAN helps to reduce the acidity of the soil while releasing nitrogen gradually, thereby reducing the risk of nitrogen loss through leaching.</p><p>The Limestone Ammonium Nitrate (LAN) market is estimated to witness substantial growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for high crop yield and the growing need for efficient fertilizers are key factors driving the market growth. LAN offers several advantages, such as its slow-release properties, reduced environmental impact, and enhanced nutrient availability for crops, which further contribute to its market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, increasing government initiatives to promote sustainable agriculture practices and the adoption of advanced farming techniques are expected to fuel market growth.
