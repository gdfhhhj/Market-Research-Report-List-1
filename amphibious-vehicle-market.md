<p><p>The amphibious vehicle market is categorized into three types based on their propulsion mechanisms. The first type is the screw propeller propulsion market, where vehicles are equipped with rotating screw propellers to provide thrust in both water and land. The second type is the water jet propulsion market, which utilizes high-pressure water jets to propel the vehicles in water. The third type is the track-based propulsion market, where vehicles have tracks similar to tanks, allowing them to move in various terrains, including water. These propulsion types offer different advantages and cater to different needs in the amphibious vehicle market.</p></p>
