<p><p>The commercial aerospace landing gear market consists of different types, including the actuation system and the steering system. The actuation system is responsible for deploying and retracting the landing gear during takeoff and landing. It utilizes hydraulic or electro-hydraulic mechanisms to operate the gear. On the other hand, the steering system enables the aircraft to steer while on the ground. This system allows for smooth taxiing, maneuvering, and parking. Both these systems are crucial in ensuring safe and efficient aircraft operations.</p></p>
