<p><strong>The Commercial Undercounter Glasswasher Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Waste Type</li><li>Pumped Waste Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial undercounter glasswasher market consists of two main types: gravity waste type and pumped waste type. In the gravity waste type, the wastewater flows out due to gravity, without the need for a pump. This type is suitable for installations where the wastewater outlet is at a lower elevation than the glasswasher. On the other hand, the pumped waste type utilizes a pump to expel the wastewater, making it suitable for installations where the wastewater outlet is at a higher elevation than the glasswasher.</p></p>
