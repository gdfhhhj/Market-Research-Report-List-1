<p><p>The Semiconductor Thinner market consists of two types: Positive Photoresist Removal and Negative Photoresist Removal. Positive Photoresist Removal involves the elimination of unwanted material from semiconductor surfaces that have been exposed to UV light. This process helps create desired patterns on the surface. On the other hand, Negative Photoresist Removal involves the removal of unwanted material from the surface that was not exposed to UV light. Both types play a vital role in the semiconductor industry by ensuring the precise and accurate fabrication of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
