<p><strong>The Blotting Reagents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blotting reagents are substances used in blotting techniques, such as Western blotting, Northern blotting, and Southern blotting. Type I blotting reagents refer to the ones used for protein detection, including primary and secondary antibodies. These reagents specifically bind to target proteins for identification. Type II blotting reagents are employed for nucleic acid detection. They consist of probes or primers that bind to specific DNA or RNA sequences, allowing for the visualization and analysis of genetic material. Both Type I and Type II reagents play essential roles in molecular biology research.</p></p>
