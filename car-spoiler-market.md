<p><p>The car spoiler market is categorized into three types: glass fiber reinforced plastics (GRP), aluminium alloy, and carbon fibre. Glass fiber reinforced plastics spoilers are made from a composite material consisting of fine glass fibers embedded in a polymer matrix, providing a balance between strength and weight. Aluminium alloy spoilers are lighter and offer good durability and corrosion resistance. Carbon fibre spoilers are the premium option, known for their high strength-to-weight ratio, aerodynamic properties, and sleek appearance, making them popular among performance car enthusiasts.</p></p>
