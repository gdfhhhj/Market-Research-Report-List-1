<p><ul><li>Water-based Drilling Fluids</li><li>Oil-based Drilling Fluids</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Filtration control additives are used in both water-based and oil-based drilling fluids to control the filtration rate of the fluid during drilling operations. In water-based drilling fluids, these additives help to prevent the loss of fluid into the formation, improving wellbore stability and minimizing formation damage. In oil-based drilling fluids, filtration control additives play a crucial role in controlling the fluid's rheology and filter cake buildup, ensuring efficient hole cleaning and preventing differential sticking. These additives are essential for maintaining drilling efficiency and overall well integrity.</p></p>
