<p><strong>The Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based CFRTP</li><li>Pitch-based CFRTP</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Carbon Fiber Reinforced Thermoplastic Market comprises two main types: Polyacrylonitrile (PAN)-based CFRTP and Pitch-based CFRTP. PAN-based CFRTP is manufactured using a polymer called polyacrylonitrile, which goes through various processes like oxidation and carbonization to produce carbon fibers. This type of CFRTP offers high strength and stiffness, making it suitable for applications where lightweight and robust materials are required. On the other hand, Pitch-based CFRTP is produced using a different carbon precursor called petroleum pitch. It provides excellent heat and chemical resistance, making it suitable for industries such as aerospace and automotive.</p></p>
