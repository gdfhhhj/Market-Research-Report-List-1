<p><p>The modular laser cutting machine market is categorized into three types: CNC (computer numerical control), manual control, and PLC (programmable logic controller) control. CNC-controlled machines use advanced software to precisely execute cutting patterns. They offer high accuracy, speed, and automation. Manual control machines require human intervention to operate and adjust the cutting process. PLC control machines utilize programmable logic controllers to automate and regulate the cutting operations, ensuring consistency and efficiency in production. These different types provide flexibility and options for manufacturers based on their specific needs and requirements.</p></p>
