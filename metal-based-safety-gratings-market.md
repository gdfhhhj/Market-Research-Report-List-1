<p><p>The metal-based safety gratings market includes various types of gratings designed for safety purposes. Flat type grating is a plain surface grating used in applications where slip resistance is not a major concern. Serrated type grating features a grooved surface that offers enhanced grip and slip resistance. Press-locked steel bar grating consists of flat bars that are locked together by pressure welding, resulting in a strong and durable grating. These different types cater to diverse safety requirements in industries such as construction, oil and gas, and manufacturing.</p></p>
