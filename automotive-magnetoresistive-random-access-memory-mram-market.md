<p><p>Automotive Magnetoresistive Random Access Memory (MRAM) market comprises two major types: Toggle MRAM and STT-MRAM. Toggle MRAM technology utilizes a "magnetic tunnel junction" structure, ensuring higher size scalability, faster write speeds, and lower power consumption, making it suitable for automotive applications. On the other hand, STT-MRAM employs spin transfer torque, enabling higher density and capacity, lower power consumption, and non-volatility. Both types offer advantages in terms of performance and power efficiency, catering to the specific needs of the automotive industry's memory requirements.</p></p>
