<p><p>Off-road motorcycle helmets are available in three different types: ABS, Glass Fiber Reinforced Plastic (GRP), and Carbon Fiber. ABS helmets are made of a strong thermoplastic material that offers good impact resistance and durability. GRP helmets are constructed using a mixture of plastic and glass fibers, resulting in a lightweight yet sturdy design. Carbon Fiber helmets use a combination of carbon fibers and resin to create an extremely lightweight and strong helmet. These different types of helmets provide riders with options based on their preferences and requirements for protection while off-roading.</p></p>
