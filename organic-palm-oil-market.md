<p><p>The organic palm oil market consists of two main types: crude palm oil and palm olein. Crude palm oil is a basic form of palm oil obtained from the fruit's mesocarp. It is commonly used in food products, cosmetics, and biodiesel production. On the other hand, palm olein is the liquid fraction of palm oil obtained after fractionation. It is used in cooking oils, frying fats, and as an ingredient in various food products. Both market types play a significant role in the organic palm oil industry.</p></p>
