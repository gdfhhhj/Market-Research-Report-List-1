<p><p>Iron and steel slag processing refers to the process used for recovering and processing slag produced during iron and steel manufacturing. <p><p>The iron and steel slag processing market encompasses various methods for processing slag generated during iron and steel production. The hot splash method involves quenching the molten slag with water or steam, resulting in fragmentation and granulation. The hot stuffy method involves cooling the slag slowly to produce crystalline slag. The roller method uses rollers to crush and grind the slag into a fine powder. Additionally, there are other methods available in the market, each with their own unique processes and benefits for slag processing.</p></p>
