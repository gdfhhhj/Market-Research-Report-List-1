<p><p>The remote starter market is classified into two main types: one-way remote starter and two-way remote starter. One-way remote starters allow users to start the car's engine and unlock/lock the doors from a distance. However, they do not provide any confirmation or feedback about the car's current status. In contrast, two-way remote starters provide users with real-time feedback on the car's status, confirming if the engine has started or if the doors are locked/unlocked. This additional communication feature makes two-way remote starters more advanced and preferred by many users.</p></p>
