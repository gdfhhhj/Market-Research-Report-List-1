<p><p>The two-wheeler instrument cluster market can be categorized into three types: analog, digital, and hybrid. Analog instrument clusters feature traditional dial-based displays, providing essential vehicle information such as speed, fuel level, and engine RPM. Digital clusters utilize digital screens to show information in a more customizable and visually appealing manner, often allowing personalization options. Hybrid clusters combine analog and digital elements, offering a balance between the classic look of analog displays and the advanced functionality of digital screens. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and provides varying levels of information display capabilities.</p></p>
