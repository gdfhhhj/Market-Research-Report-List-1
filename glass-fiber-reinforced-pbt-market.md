Injection Molding involves the process of melting the Glass Fiber Reinforced PBT resin and injecting it into a mold to form various parts and components. Extrusion Molding, on the other hand, requires the heated resin to be forced through a die to create continuous shapes like pipes, tubes, or sheets.
