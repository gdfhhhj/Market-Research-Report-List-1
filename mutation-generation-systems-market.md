<p><p>The Mutation Generation Systems market includes various types of mutations occurring in DNA. Missense Mutation refers to a single alteration in the genetic code, resulting in a different amino acid. Nonsense Mutation leads to a premature stop codon, resulting in a shorter protein. Insertion and Deletion involve the addition or removal of genetic material, respectively. Duplication refers to the presence of extra copies of certain genes. Frame Shift Mutation occurs due to adding or deleting nucleotides, altering the entire protein sequence. Repeat Expansion is the repeat of certain nucleotide sequences resulting in genetic disorders.</p></p>
