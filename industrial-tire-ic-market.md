<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial tire market consists of two main types: bias tire and radial tire. Bias tires are constructed with multiple plies of rubber and fabric cords laid at opposing angles. These tires are known for their durability and resistance to impacts. On the other hand, radial tires have steel belts running under the tread, resulting in improved stability and reduced rolling resistance. Both types serve specific purposes in industries such as construction, agriculture, and mining, with bias tires being preferred for heavy-duty off-road applications, and radial tires offering better performance on smoother terrain.</p></p>
