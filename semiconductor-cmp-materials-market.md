<p><ul><li>CMP Pads</li><li>CMP Slurries</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Semiconductor CMP materials are used in chemical mechanical planarization (CMP) processes, which is essential for manufacturing microchips. CMP pads are a type of material that is placed on the CMP machine to provide a smooth surface for the polishing process. They help in achieving a high level of planarity and reducing defects on the chip surface. On the other hand, CMP slurries are abrasive liquids containing small particles that are used to remove excess material from the wafer surface during the CMP process. These slurries play a crucial role in achieving precise and uniform polishing results for semiconductor wafers.</p></p>
