<p><p>A chipboard box, also known as a carton or paperboard box, is a type of packaging made from recycled paperboard. Firstly, the increasing demand for sustainable packaging solutions is driving the adoption of chipboard boxes as they are made from recyclable materials. Additionally, the growth of the e-commerce industry and the need for secure packaging to prevent damage during transit are fueling the demand for chipboard boxes.</p><p>Furthermore, the rise in urbanization and the expansion of the retail sector are also driving the demand for chipboard boxes. The convenience and cost-effectiveness of chipboard boxes make them an ideal choice for packaging various products, including food and beverages, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and consumer electronics.</p><p>However, the chipboard box market faces challenges such as the availability of alternatives like plastic packaging and regulatory restrictions on the use of certain materials.
