<p><p>The spring strip steel market includes two main types: hot rolled and cold rolled. It is generally used for applications that require strong and durable materials. Cold rolled spring strip steel, on the other hand, is produced at room temperature through a process that involves rolling the steel to the desired thickness. It is typically used for applications that require smoother surface finishes and tighter tolerances.</p></p>
