<p><p>Bed bug sprays can be classified into two main market types: synthetic and natural. Synthetic bed bug sprays are formulated using man-made chemicals that are specifically designed to kill or repel bed bugs. These products may contain ingredients such as pyrethroids or pyrethrin that are known to be effective against bed bugs. On the other hand, natural bed bug sprays are made from organic or plant-based ingredients. These products often use essential oils like lavender or tea tree oil, which are believed to have insect-repelling properties. Both synthetic and natural bed bug sprays aim to eliminate or deter bed bug infestations, but they differ in the composition of their active ingredients.</p></p>
