<p><p>The glasses-free 3D HD displays market includes different types of technologies. Light barrier technology uses a series of barriers to direct the left and right images to corresponding eyes. Lenticular lens technology employs a lens array to send different images to the left and right eyes. Directional backlight involves a backlight panel that emits light at different angles for creating a 3D effect. Direct imaging utilizes special pixels that emit light at various angles. Other types include parallax barriers, autostereoscopic technology, and holographic displays. These technologies aim to provide a 3D viewing experience without the need for wearing glasses.</p></p>
