<p><p>Urethane surface coatings are used to protect and enhance the appearance of various surfaces. The market offers three types of urethane surface coatings: powder, solvent-borne, and water-borne. Powder coatings are applied as dry powder and cured through a heating process, providing a durable and high-quality finish. Solvent-borne coatings are solvent-based and offer excellent adhesion and durability but release harmful chemicals. Water-borne coatings use water as a base and have lower VOC emissions, making them more environmentally friendly. Each type caters to different customer needs and preferences.</p></p>
