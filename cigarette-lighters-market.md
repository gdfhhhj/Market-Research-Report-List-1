<p><ul><li>Liquid Lighters</li><li>Gaseous Lighters</li><li>Gas-liquid Mixing Lighters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cigarette lighters come in various types, including liquid lighters, gaseous lighters, and gas-liquid mixing lighters. Liquid lighters use a liquid fuel, typically lighter fluid, and produce a flame by igniting the liquid. Gaseous lighters store a pressurized gas, such as butane, which is ignited to create a flame. Gas-liquid mixing lighters combine both liquid fuel and gas, resulting in a flame produced by a mixture of the two. These different types cater to individual preferences and requirements of users.</p></p>
