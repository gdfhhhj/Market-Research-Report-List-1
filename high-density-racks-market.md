<p><strong>The High-Density Racks Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drive-in Rack</li><li>Drive-through Rack</li></ul></p>
<p><p>High-Density Racks refer to storage systems that maximize warehouse space by utilizing vertical storage. The Drive-in Rack type involves last-in, first-out (LIFO) storage, where pallets are loaded on rails and pushed back, allowing forklifts to access and retrieve items from the front. Drive-through Rack operates on a first-in, first-out (FIFO) basis, offering easy access to pallets from both ends of the rack. Both market types are designed for high-density storage, enabling efficient storage and retrieval of goods while optimizing warehouse space.</p></p>
