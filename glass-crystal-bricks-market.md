<p><p>Glass crystal bricks are a type of decorative building material used in construction and interior design. They are available in two main market types: solid and hollow. Solid crystal bricks are made from solid glass, providing strength and durability. They are commonly used for load-bearing purposes or as structural elements in buildings. On the other hand, hollow crystal bricks have empty spaces inside, making them lighter and easier to handle. They are often used for non-load-bearing applications, such as decorative walls or partitions, where their transparency and light diffusion properties can create stunning visual effects.</p></p>
