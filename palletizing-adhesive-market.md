<p><p>The Palletizing Adhesive Market includes two main types: Hot Melt Palletizing Adhesive and Spray Palletizing Adhesive. Hot Melt Palletizing Adhesive refers to a type of adhesive that is melted and applied in a molten state. It provides excellent bonding strength and fast curing time, making it suitable for high-speed palletizing applications. On the other hand, Spray Palletizing Adhesive is applied through a spray nozzle, providing an even and uniform coating. This type of adhesive is preferred in industries where precise and controlled application is required.</p></p>
