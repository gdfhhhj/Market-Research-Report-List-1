Cadmium-based batteries, such as nickel-cadmium (Ni-Cd) batteries, are known for their high energy density and long life, making them popular in portable devices and electric vehicles.</p><p>Additionally, the rising demand for pigments and coatings in industries like paints, plastics, and ceramics is anticipated to boost the cadmium market. Cadmium-based pigments offer excellent color stability, heat resistance, and durability, making them ideal for various applications. Furthermore, the use of cadmium in electroplating and metal coatings is expected to contribute to market growth as these processes are extensively utilized in the manufacturing sector.</p><p>However, despite the positive outlook, the cadmium market may face challenges due to environmental concerns and regulations.
