<p><p>The Fire Alarm Devices Market includes two main types of signaling equipment: visual signaling equipment and audible signaling equipment. </p><p>Visual signaling equipment is designed to alert individuals through the use of lights or strobes, providing a clear and visible indication of a fire alarm. This can be especially helpful for individuals with hearing impairments or in environments with high noise levels. </p><p>On the other hand, audible signaling equipment produces loud and distinct sounds or sirens to warn people of a fire. These alarms are effective in areas with low visibility or where visual alarms may not be sufficient.</p></p>
