<p><strong>The Terahertz Imaging Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Terahertz Imaging</li><li>Active Terahertz Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Terahertz imaging devices are divided into two types: passive terahertz imaging and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging devices capture natural terahertz radiation emitted by objects to create an image. They are beneficial for security screening as they can detect concealed objects without harmful radiation. On the other hand, active terahertz imaging devices emit terahertz waves towards objects and analyze the reflected signals to create an image. Active devices offer higher resolution and can penetrate non-metallic materials, making them suitable for industrial inspection and medical imaging.</p></p>
