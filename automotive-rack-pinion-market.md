<p><p>Automotive rack and pinion is a steering mechanism commonly used in automobiles. One of the key factors driving the market growth is the increasing production and sales of automobiles worldwide. As the automotive industry continues to expand, so will the demand for steering systems like rack and pinion. Additionally, the rising trend of electric vehicles (EVs) is expected to further drive the demand for rack and pinion systems, as they are often preferred in electric power-assisted steering (EPAS) systems.</p><p>Moreover, advancements in technology are also anticipated to play a significant role in the growth of the market. Manufacturers are focusing on developing lightweight and compact rack and pinion systems that offer improved efficiency and precision.
