<p><strong>The Automotive Digital Rear View Mirror Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Body Mounted</li><li>Door Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive digital rear-view mirror market includes two types: body mounted and door mounted. Body mounted digital rear-view mirrors are integrated into the body of the vehicle, usually at the top center of the windshield. These mirrors use advanced cameras to capture real-time images of the surroundings and display them on a display screen inside the vehicle. On the other hand, door mounted digital rear-view mirrors are located on the exterior of the vehicle, specifically on the driver's or passenger's side door. They function similarly to body mounted mirrors but provide a wider field of view, enhancing the driver's visibility.</p></p>
