<p><p>Handwriting Recognition (HWR) refers to the technology that enables computers to analyze and interpret human handwriting. The HWR market consists of two main types: Offline Handwriting Recognition and Online Handwriting Recognition. Offline HWR processes handwritten data offline, usually by scanning a document and converting it into digital text for further analysis. On the other hand, Online HWR recognizes and interprets handwriting in real-time, allowing users to write directly on electronic devices such as tablets or touch screens. Both types of HWR offer diverse applications across industries, including document digitization, digital note-taking, and forms processing.</p></p>
