<p><p>The endometrial biopsy cannulae market includes two main types: endometrial biopsy brush and endometrial biopsy catheter. An endometrial biopsy brush is a device used to collect tissue samples from the lining of the uterus. It consists of a brush-like tip that is inserted into the uterus and rotated to collect the sample. On the other hand, an endometrial biopsy catheter is a thin, flexible tube that is inserted into the uterus to collect tissue samples. Both these types of cannulae are commonly used in gynecological procedures for diagnosing various conditions of the uterus.</p></p>
