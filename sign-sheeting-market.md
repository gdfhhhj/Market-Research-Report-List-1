<p><p>Sign sheeting is a material used to enhance the visibility of road signs and other traffic devices. There are two main types of sign sheeting available in the market. Glass bead type sheeting uses tiny reflective glass beads embedded in the surface, which reflect light back to its source. This type is commonly used in lower visibility conditions. On the other hand, micro prismatic type sheeting consists of small prismatic shapes that reflect light in all directions, resulting in increased visibility. This type is typically used in brighter conditions or where higher retroreflectivity is required.</p></p>
