<p><p>The automotive electric drive axle market consists of two main types: electric axles and hybrid axles. Electric axles are designed to solely rely on electricity for power transmission, using electric motors to drive the wheels. On the other hand, hybrid axles combine both electric and conventional power sources, typically featuring both an electric motor and an internal combustion engine. While electric axles facilitate emissions-free driving, hybrid axles offer the flexibility of utilizing both electricity and gasoline, providing enhanced efficiency and range in vehicles.</p></p>
