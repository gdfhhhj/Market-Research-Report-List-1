<p><p>A Voice over Long-term Evolution (VoLTE) smartwatch is a wearable device that supports voice calling over high-speed 4G LTE networks. With advancements in communication technologies and increasing consumer demand for wearable devices, the adoption of VoLTE smartwatches is expected to grow significantly.
