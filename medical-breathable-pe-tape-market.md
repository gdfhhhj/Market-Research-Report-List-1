<p><p>The Medical Breathable PE Tape market includes two main types: LDPE and HDPE. LDPE refers to low-density polyethylene, which is a type of plastic that is flexible and has good resistance to moisture. LDPE tapes are commonly used in medical applications where breathability is important, such as wound dressing and surgical bandages. On the other hand, HDPE stands for high-density polyethylene, which is a stronger and more rigid plastic. HDPE tapes are used for applications that require durability and strength, such as securing medical tubing or equipment.</p></p>
