<p><strong>The Automotive Injector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-point injection</li><li>Continuous injection</li><li>Sequential injection</li><li>Direct injection</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive injector market is comprised of various types including single-point injection, continuous injection, sequential injection, direct injection, and others. Single-point injection systems introduce fuel into the engine through one injector, ensuring fuel distribution across all cylinders. Continuous injection involves a constant fuel flow into the engine, allowing for precise control of the fuel-air mixture. Sequential injection systems deliver fuel to each cylinder individually, optimizing combustion efficiency. Direct injection injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber, improving fuel efficiency and power. The "others" category includes alternative injection technologies like port fuel injection and throttle body injection.</p></p>
