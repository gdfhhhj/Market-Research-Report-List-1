<p><strong>The Intelligent Cash Registers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>All-In-One</li><li>Pos Touch Machine</li><li>Pos Cash Register</li><li>Electronic Cash Register</li><li>Split Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Intelligent cash registers are advanced systems used by businesses to manage sales transactions and inventory. All-In-One cash registers combine a cash drawer, receipt printer, and touchscreen display in a single device. Pos Touch Machines are similar, but offer more features like barcode scanning and customer display. Pos Cash Registers are simpler, offering basic transaction processing and often require separate peripherals. Electronic Cash Registers are traditional machines that record sales with manual input. Split Type registers separate the display and cash drawer, allowing for more flexible placement in stores or restaurants.</p></p>
