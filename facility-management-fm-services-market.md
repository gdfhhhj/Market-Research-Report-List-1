<p><p>Facility Management (FM) Services market can be divided into two main types: Soft Services and Hard Services. Soft services refer to the non-physical tasks that focus on the comfort and well-being of building occupants. This includes services like cleaning, catering, security, waste management, and landscaping. On the other hand, Hard Services involve the physical tasks required to maintain and operate a facility efficiently. These services typically include maintenance and repair of electrical systems, plumbing, HVAC, and other mechanical systems. Both categories are essential for effective facility management and contribute to the overall functioning and productivity of a facility.</p></p>
