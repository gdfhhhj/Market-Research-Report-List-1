<p><p>The Soybean Peptide Powder market can be classified into three types: endogenous peptide powder, synthetic peptide powder, and enzymatic peptide powder. Endogenous peptide powder is derived naturally from soybeans and is rich in bioactive peptides. Synthetic peptide powder is produced in a laboratory using chemical synthesis methods to mimic the properties of natural peptides. Enzymatic peptide powder is obtained through enzymatic hydrolysis, where enzymes are used to break down proteins in soybeans and generate peptides with specific functions. Each type of peptide powder offers different characteristics and uses in various industries.</p></p>
