<p><p>The insulated cable market comprises two major types: XLPE insulated cable and PVC insulated cable. XLPE insulated cable, also known as cross-linked polyethylene, is characterized by high temperature resistance, excellent electrical properties, and durability. It is mainly used in power transmission and distribution networks. PVC insulated cable, on the other hand, refers to cables that have a polyvinyl chloride insulation layer. PVC insulated cables are widely used for various applications including building wiring, electrical appliances, and automotive industries due to their cost-effectiveness and flexibility.</p></p>
