<p><p>High-Strength Acrylic Adhesives market is classified into four types: Water-based, Solvent-based, Reactive, and Other. Water-based acrylic adhesives contain water as the main solvent, making them eco-friendly and easy to clean. Solvent-based adhesives use solvents like acetone or toluene, providing excellent adhesion but emitting harmful VOCs. Reactive acrylic adhesives are cured through a chemical reaction, offering strong bonds and durability. The "Other" category comprises unique formulations or combinations of the aforementioned types, providing specialized properties such as fast setting or high temperature resistance.</p></p>
