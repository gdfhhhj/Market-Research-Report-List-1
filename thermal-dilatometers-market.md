<p><p>Thermal dilatometers are devices used to measure the thermal expansion of materials. There are three main types of thermal dilatometers on the market. Capacitance thermal dilatometers utilize changes in capacitance to measure the expansion of a material. Connecting rod (push rod) thermal dilatometers use a push rod to measure the expansion and contraction of a material. Optical thermal dilatometers rely on changes in light intensity to measure the expansion. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different applications depending on the desired level of accuracy and measurement range.</p></p>
