<p><p>The automobile brake booster pumps market is categorized into three types: pneumatic booster pump, oil brake booster pump, and other. Pneumatic booster pumps use compressed air to enhance the force applied to the brake pedal, resulting in better braking performance. Oil brake booster pumps use hydraulic fluid to amplify the force, providing efficient braking. The "other" category encompasses various brake booster pump types, such as electric and vacuum pumps, which contribute to brake system enhancement in automobiles.</p></p>
