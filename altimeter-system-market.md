<p><strong>The Altimeter System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Barometric Leveling</li><li>Laser Altimetry</li><li>Radar Altimetry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Altimeter System Market comprises various types of technologies used to measure altitude. Barometric Leveling is a method that uses atmospheric pressure to determine altitude. Laser Altimetry employs laser beams to measure the distance between the sensor and the ground, enabling altitude calculation. Radar Altimetry utilizes radio waves to measure the distance from the aircraft to the ground surface, thus determining the altitude. These technologies play a crucial role in applications such as aviation, meteorology, and geodetic surveys, providing accurate altitude measurements in different contexts.</p></p>
