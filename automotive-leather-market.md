<p><p>The automotive leather market consists of two main types: genuine leather and synthetic leather. Genuine leather is made from the hide of animals like cows and buffaloes, providing a luxurious and natural feel. It offers excellent durability and breathability. On the other hand, synthetic leather, also known as faux or artificial leather, is composed of man-made materials like PVC or PU. It imitates the look and texture of real leather but at a lower cost. Synthetic leather is chosen for its water and fade resistance, and it offers a wider range of design options.</p></p>
