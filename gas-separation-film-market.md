<p><ul><li>Hollow Fiber</li><li>Spiral Wound</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gas separation film market is classified into three main types: hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber films consist of numerous small hollow fibers, offering a large surface area for gas separation. Spiral wound films consist of layers of a membrane and spacer, wound tightly together in a spiral form, allowing gas to pass through the membrane. The "others" category includes various types of gas separation films that don't fall into the previous two categories. These films play a crucial role in industries such as petrochemicals, oil and gas, and oxygen generation.</p></p>
