<p><p>The screen door system market comprises two main types: full height and half height systems. Full height screen door systems are designed to cover the entire height of an entrance, providing a complete barrier against insects, dust, and other elements while allowing for easy passage. On the other hand, half height screen door systems are designed to cover only a portion of the entrance, typically waist-high, offering a lower level of protection while still allowing for ventilation and interaction between spaces.
