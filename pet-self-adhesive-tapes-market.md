On the other hand, Oil Based Adhesive Tape is manufactured using adhesives derived from petroleum, providing excellent adhesion and resistance to moisture. The choice between these types depends on specific needs, with Water Based Adhesive Tape being favored for its eco-friendly properties, while Oil Based Adhesive Tape is preferred for its durability and water-resistance.</p></p>
