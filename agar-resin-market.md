<p><p>Agar resin is available in three different types: powder, granules, and strips. Powder form refers to finely ground agar resin, which is commonly used in the preparation of agar plates for laboratory purposes. Granules are larger particles of agar resin, typically used in food and pharmaceutical industries for their gelling and stabilizing properties. Strips are solid, thin pieces of agar resin, often used in bacteriological studies or in the cultivation of microorganisms. These different forms cater to various applications and enable users to choose the most suitable product for their needs.</p></p>
