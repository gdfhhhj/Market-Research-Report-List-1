<p><p>Thermoplastic Polyolefin (TPO) membranes are single-ply roofing membranes that are widely used in commercial and residential buildings for waterproofing purposes. TPO membranes are made from a blend of polypropylene (PP) and ethylene-propylene rubber (EPR) and they possess excellent weathering resistance, flexibility, and durability. TPO membranes are highly popular due to their low cost, ease of installation, and energy-efficient properties.</p><p>The global Thermoplastic Polyolefin (TPO) membranes market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to various factors such as the growing construction industry, increasing demand for energy-efficient roofing systems, and stringent government regulations for building energy conservation. One of the major trends is the increasing adoption of TPO membranes in green roofing systems, as they help reduce the energy consumption of buildings and contribute to environmental sustainability.
