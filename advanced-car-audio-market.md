<p><p>The Advanced Car Audio market consists of two main types: OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and Aftermarket. OEM refers to the audio systems installed by car manufacturers during the production stage. These systems are specifically designed to match the car's specifications and are often considered of higher quality. On the other hand, Aftermarket audio products are those installed in a vehicle after it has been purchased. These products offer a wider range of options and customization for audio enthusiasts, allowing them to upgrade or replace the OEM audio systems.</p></p>
