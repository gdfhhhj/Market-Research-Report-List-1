<p><p>The market for transparent conductors using silver nanomaterials can be classified into four types: gravure printing, offset printing, screen printing, and inkjet printing. Gravure printing involves engraving the image onto a metal plate, which then transfers the ink onto the substrate. Offset printing utilizes a rubber blanket to transfer the ink from a printing plate onto the substrate. Screen printing involves a mesh screen that blocks the ink except where the image is intended to be printed. Inkjet printing uses droplets of ink deposited onto the substrate. These printing methods facilitate the production of transparent conductive films with silver nanomaterials.</p></p>
