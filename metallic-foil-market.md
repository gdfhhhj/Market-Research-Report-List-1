<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The metallic foil market consists of two main types: hot stamping foil and cold stamping foil. Hot stamping foil is a type that requires a hot press or machine to transfer the foil onto a surface. It is commonly used in industries like packaging and printing. On the other hand, cold stamping foil does not require heat for transfer and is more suitable for surfaces that may be sensitive to heat, such as paper products or plastics. Both types offer a range of metallic finishes and are widely used for decorative and branding purposes.</p></p>
