<p><p>A 3D CBCT (Cone Beam Computed Tomography) machine is a medical imaging device used to produce three-dimensional images of dental and maxillofacial structures. These images are then reconstructed into a three-dimensional volume, providing detailed information for diagnosis and treatment planning in dentistry.</p><p>The 3D CBCT machine market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 9.6% during the forecast period. <p><strong>The 3D CBCT Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Large FOV</li><li>Medium FOV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D CBCT machine market can be categorized into large FOV and medium FOV types based on their field of view. Large FOV machines offer a wider coverage area for capturing detailed images of the entire maxillofacial region, including the jaw and sinus areas. These machines are commonly used in oral and maxillofacial surgery and implant planning. On the other hand, medium FOV machines have a smaller coverage area, focusing primarily on the dental arch. They are widely used in general dentistry for diagnosing tooth-related issues and planning orthodontic treatments.</p></p>
