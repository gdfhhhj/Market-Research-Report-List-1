<p><p>The automotive engine radiators market can be divided into two types: aluminum core radiators and copper core radiators. Aluminum core radiators consist of an aluminum core surrounded by aluminum tanks, offering excellent heat transfer and lightweight advantages. Copper core radiators, on the other hand, have a copper core surrounded by copper tanks, providing efficient heat dissipation and superior durability. Both types are used in automotive engines to prevent overheating and maintain optimal operating temperatures, but they differ in terms of material composition and specific benefits.</p></p>
