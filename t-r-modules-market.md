<p><p>T-R Modules, or transmit-receive modules, are an essential component in various wireless communication systems. There are three main market types for T-R modules: millimeter wave, centimeter wave, and other markets. Millimeter wave T-R modules operate at high frequencies between 30 to 300 GHz, providing high-speed data transmission for applications like 5G networks and autonomous vehicles. Centimeter wave modules cover frequencies ranging from 10 to 100 GHz, offering long-range communication capabilities for radar systems and satellite communications. The "other" market encompasses modules operating outside the millimeter and centimeter wave bands, supporting diverse wireless applications such as Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and cellular networks.</p></p>
