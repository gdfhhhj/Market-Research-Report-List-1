<p><p>Flame retardants are chemicals used in various industries to prevent or delay the spread of fire. There are several types of flame retardants available in the market, including ATH (aluminum trihydrate), Antimony Oxide, Brominated, Chlorinated, and Phosphorous flame retardants. ATH is widely used due to its low toxicity and high effectiveness in reducing flames. Antimony Oxide is commonly used in combination with other flame retardants to enhance fire resistance. Brominated and Chlorinated flame retardants contain halogens and are effective in preventing fire spread. Phosphorous flame retardants are environmentally friendly alternatives to brominated and chlorinated variants.</p></p>
