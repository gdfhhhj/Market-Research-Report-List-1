<p><p>The commercial vehicle gasoline engine intake valve market consists of three main types: mono metallic valve, bimetallic valve, and hollow valve. Mono metallic valves are made from a single material, typically steel or iron. Bimetallic valves, on the other hand, are composed of two different materials, such as steel and brass. Hollow valves have a hollow center, which helps in reducing the valve weight. These different types of valves cater to varying customer demands and offer unique advantages in terms of strength, durability, and weight reduction for commercial vehicle engines.</p></p>
