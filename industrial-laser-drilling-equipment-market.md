<p><p>The Industrial Laser Drilling Equipment Market is divided into two major types: Pulsed Fiber Laser and CW Fiber Laser. Pulsed Fiber Lasers emit high-intensity laser beams in short pulses, which are very effective in drilling hard materials. They are often used for high-precision applications where accuracy and control are crucial. On the other hand, CW (Continuous Wave) Fiber Lasers emit a constant laser beam and are ideal for drilling soft or fragile materials that require a continuous and sustained heat source. Both types have their applications based on the material and precision needed.</p></p>
