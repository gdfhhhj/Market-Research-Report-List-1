<p><p>The dog vaccines market includes various types of vaccines that are used to prevent diseases in dogs. Attenuated live vaccines contain weakened versions of the disease-causing microorganisms. Conjugate vaccines combine antigens from the microorganism with a carrier protein. Inactivated vaccines use killed microorganisms to stimulate an immune response. Subunit vaccines contain only specific parts of the microorganism. Toxoid vaccines are made from the toxins produced by the microorganism. DNA vaccines introduce genetically engineered DNA to produce an immune response. Recombinant vaccines use genetic engineering to produce antigens that stimulate an immune response.</p></p>
