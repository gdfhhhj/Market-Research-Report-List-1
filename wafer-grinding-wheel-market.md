<p><p>The wafer grinding wheel market is divided into two types: coarse grind and fine grind. The coarse grind segment comprises grinding wheels with larger abrasive particles, designed for removing thicker layers of material from wafers. This type is typically used for initial wafer thinning. On the other hand, the fine grind segment consists of grinding wheels with smaller abrasive particles, which provide a smoother surface finish and precision in wafer thickness. Fine grind wheels are commonly employed for final thinning or polishing processes, ensuring the desired wafer thickness and quality.</p></p>
