<p><p>Agriculture drippers are devices used in irrigation systems to deliver water to plants. There are two types of agriculture drippers available in the market - pressure compensating drippers and non-pressure compensated drippers. Pressure compensating drippers ensure a consistent flow of water, even in areas with varying water pressure. On the other hand, non-pressure compensated drippers deliver water based on the input pressure, which may result in uneven water distribution. Both types have their own advantages and suitability depending on the specific irrigation needs and conditions.</p></p>
