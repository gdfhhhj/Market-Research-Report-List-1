<p><p>The Aluminium Welding Wire Market can be categorized into three types: Pure Aluminum Welding Wire, Al-Mg Alloy Welding Wire, and Al-Si Alloy Welding Wire. Pure Aluminum Welding Wire is made up of 99.6% pure aluminum and is primarily used for joining non-heat-treatable alloys. Al-Mg Alloy Welding Wire contains magnesium, providing enhanced strength and corrosion resistance, making it suitable for marine applications. Al-Si Alloy Welding Wire contains silicon, resulting in improved fluidity and crack resistance, commonly used for automotive components. Overall, these different types cater to diverse welding requirements in various industries.</p></p>
