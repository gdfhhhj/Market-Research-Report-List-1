<p><p>Rotary solenoids are electromechanical devices that convert electrical energy into rotary motion. They find diverse applications across various industries. Bistable rotary solenoids have two stable positions due to their magnetic structure, which makes them ideal for applications requiring power-saving or precise positioning. Monostable rotary solenoids have only one stable position and are commonly used in automation, automotive, and medical devices. Self-restoring rotary solenoids return to their neutral position when the power is turned off, ideal for safety applications. ON-OFF rotary solenoids provide simple, binary rotational motion. Other types of rotary solenoids include adjustable stroke, multi-positioning, and proportional control solenoids, offering specific functionalities for different applications.</p></p>
