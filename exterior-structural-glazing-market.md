<p><p>Exterior structural glazing is a market that offers different types of glass for construction purposes. Insulating glass is a type of glazing that consists of two or more glass panes separated by an air or gas-filled space, providing thermal insulation. Tempered glass is a safety glass that is strengthened through a heating and rapid cooling process, making it more resistant to breakage. Low-e glass, or low-emissivity glass, has a microscopically thin coating that reflects heat, helping to keep buildings cooler in warm climates and minimize heat loss in colder climates.</p></p>
