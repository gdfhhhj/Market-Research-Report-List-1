The LC-MS market can be categorized into different types based on the mass spectrometry configuration employed. Triple Quadrupole LC-MS is known for its high sensitivity and selectivity, making it suitable for targeted analysis. Time of Flight LC-MS provides accurate mass measurements and is often used for untargeted analysis. Quadrupole-Time of Flight LC-MS combines the advantages of both techniques. Other types of LC-MS include ion trap and Orbitrap configurations.</p></p>
