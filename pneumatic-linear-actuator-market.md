<p><p>A pneumatic linear actuator is a device that converts energy into mechanical motion in linear form, primarily by using compressed air. It comprises a pneumatic cylinder, a piston, and a valve or air control system. When air enters the cylinder, it pushes the piston, resulting in linear movement.</p><p>The pneumatic linear actuator market is witnessing significant growth due to various factors. Firstly, the increasing adoption of automation across industries is driving the demand for pneumatic linear actuators. These actuators are widely used in industries such as automotive, manufacturing, aerospace, and healthcare, among others, for applications like assembly, clamping, lifting, and material handling.</p><p>Additionally, the advantages offered by pneumatic linear actuators, such as high force, quick response time, and simple design, are contributing to their market growth.
