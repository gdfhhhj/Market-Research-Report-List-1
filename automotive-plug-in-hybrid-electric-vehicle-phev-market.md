<p><strong>The Automotive Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicle (PHEV) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Plug-in</li><li>Parallel or Hybrid Plug-in</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) come in three market types: Series Plug-in, Parallel, and Hybrid Plug-in. In a Series Plug-in PHEV, the electric motor solely drives the wheels, while the gasoline engine acts as a generator. Parallel PHEVs use both the electric motor and gasoline engine to drive the wheels simultaneously. Hybrid Plug-ins, on the other hand, primarily rely on the gasoline engine, with the electric motor providing support when needed. These market types determine how the electric and gasoline power sources are utilized in PHEVs.</p></p>
