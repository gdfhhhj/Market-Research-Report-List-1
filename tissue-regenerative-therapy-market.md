<p><p>Tissue regenerative therapy market is categorized into three types: physical stimulation regenerative, drug stimulation regenerative, and biological culture regenerative. Physical stimulation regenerative uses external forces like mechanical pressure or electrical stimulation to promote tissue regeneration. Drug stimulation regenerative involves the use of pharmaceuticals or chemical agents to stimulate tissue growth. Biological culture regenerative utilizes living cells or tissues from the patient or a donor to promote regeneration. Each type offers unique approaches to treating tissue damage and promoting healing in different medical conditions.</p></p>
