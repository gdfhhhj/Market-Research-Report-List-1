<p><p>Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy (CRT) Systems are used to treat heart failure patients by improving the coordination of heart contractions. The market includes two types: Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Pacemakers (CRT-P) and Cardiac Resynchronization Therapy Defibrillators (CRT-D). CRT-P devices deliver electrical impulses to both ventricles to synchronize contractions, while CRT-D devices also have the ability to deliver electric shocks to restore normal heart rhythm in case of life-threatening arrhythmias. Both types aim to improve the overall heart function and quality of life for heart failure patients.</p></p>
