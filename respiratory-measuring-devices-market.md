<p><p>Respiratory measuring devices are crucial tools used in healthcare settings to assess the respiratory function and diagnose respiratory diseases. Pulse Oximeters are used to measure oxygen saturation levels in the blood. Spirometers are devices that measure lung function by analyzing airflow and volume. Capnographs measure the concentration of carbon dioxide in exhaled breath, providing vital information about ventilation. Peak Flow Meters measure the maximum airflow during forced exhalation, aiding in the diagnosis and monitoring of respiratory conditions such as asthma. These devices play a significant role in managing and treating respiratory disorders.</p></p>
