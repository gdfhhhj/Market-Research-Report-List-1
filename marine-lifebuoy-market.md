<p><p>The marine lifebuoy market includes two main types: integral lifebuoys and horseshoe lifebuoys. Integral lifebuoys are designed with a circular shape and are commonly connected to a line or rail on a boat, providing immediate assistance in water emergencies. On the other hand, horseshoe lifebuoys have a horseshoe-shaped design that can be easily thrown to a person in need, but they do not have a connection to the boat. Both types are crucial safety devices used in marine environments to rescue and protect individuals in case of accidents or emergencies.</p></p>
