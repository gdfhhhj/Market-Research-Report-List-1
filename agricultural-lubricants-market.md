<p><p>The agricultural lubricants market consists of three main types: mineral lubricants, synthetic lubricants, and bio-based lubricants. Mineral lubricants are derived from crude oil and are commonly used in agricultural equipment due to their effective lubricating properties. Synthetic lubricants, on the other hand, are chemically engineered and provide superior performance and longevity. Lastly, bio-based lubricants are derived from renewable sources such as animal fats and vegetable oils, offering environmentally friendly options for farmers. Each of these lubricant types plays a crucial role in maintaining the optimal functioning of agriculture machinery and ensuring overall productivity.</p></p>
