<p><p>The Inhaled Nasal Influenza Vaccine market consists of two main types: Live Attenuated Influenza Vaccine (LAIV) and Split Influenza Vaccine (SIV). LAIV contains live but weakened flu viruses that can stimulate an immune response when administered through the nasal route. On the other hand, SIV is made from inactivated flu viruses that have been broken into pieces. Both types aim to protect against influenza viruses, but they differ in their viral composition. LAIV offers a live viral experience, while SIV provides an inactivated form to trigger immune responses.</p></p>
