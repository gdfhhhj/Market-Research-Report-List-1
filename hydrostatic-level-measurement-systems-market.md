<p><p>Hydrostatic level measurement systems are used to determine the level of liquids or bulk materials in tanks or containers. Conventional pressure transmitters use pressure sensors to measure the hydrostatic pressure at the bottom of the tank and convert it into a level measurement. Process pressure transmitters are similar but are designed for harsh environments or high-pressure applications. Submersible pressure transmitters are specifically designed to be immersed in liquid and measure the hydrostatic pressure directly. These different types cater to the varying needs of industries for accurate and reliable level measurements.</p></p>
