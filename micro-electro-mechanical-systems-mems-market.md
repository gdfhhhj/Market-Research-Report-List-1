<p><p>Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems (MEMS) refer to miniaturized mechanical and electronic devices integrated on a single chip. The MEMS market encompasses various types, including Sensing MEMS, Bio MEMS, Optical MEMS, and Radio Frequency (RF) MEMS. Sensing MEMS involve detecting and measuring physical parameters like pressure, motion, and temperature. Bio MEMS focus on medical applications, such as lab-on-a-chip technology for rapid diagnostics. Optical MEMS involve miniaturized optical devices, sensors, and micro mirrors for applications like projection displays. RF MEMS refer to devices enabling wireless communication, antenna tuning, and filters in mobile devices.</p></p>
