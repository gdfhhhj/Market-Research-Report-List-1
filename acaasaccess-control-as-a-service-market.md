<p><p>Access Control as a Service (ACaaS) is a cloud-based solution that provides access control capabilities to organizations. In the hosted market, ACaaS is offered by a third-party provider who hosts and manages the access control system on their own servers, allowing clients to access it remotely through the internet. In the managed market, ACaaS is provided by a third-party provider who manages the access control system on-premises within the client's infrastructure, offering enhanced control and customization options. Both types offer convenience, scalability, and cost-effectiveness to organizations seeking efficient access control solutions.</p></p>
