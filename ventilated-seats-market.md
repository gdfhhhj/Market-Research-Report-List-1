<p><p>Ventilated seats refer to car seats that are equipped with a built-in ventilation system to provide air circulation and airflow, enhancing comfort and reducing sweating during long drives. There are two types of ventilated seats based on the type of fans used for air circulation. Radial fans seats utilize fans that blow air radially outwards, distributing it across the seat area. On the other hand, axial fans seats use fans that pull air from one point and push it along the length of the seat. Both types offer similar benefits but differ in fan positioning and air distribution.</p></p>
