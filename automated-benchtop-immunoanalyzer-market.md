<p><p>In the automated benchtop immunoanalyzer market, there are two main types: semi-automatic and fully-automatic. A semi-automatic immunoanalyzer requires some level of human intervention, such as manual sample preparation and loading. It automates the subsequent steps including reagent dispensing, incubation, and detection. On the other hand, a fully-automatic immunoanalyzer performs all tasks from sample preparation to analysis without human intervention. This type utilizes advanced robotics and software to handle the entire immunoassay process, offering higher throughput and improved efficiency compared to semi-automatic analyzers.</p></p>
