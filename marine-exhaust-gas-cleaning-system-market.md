<p><p>The Marine Exhaust Gas Cleaning System Market comprises various types, which include Open Loop Scrubbers, Closed Loop Scrubbers, Hybrid Scrubbers, Dry Scrubbers, and Membrane Scrubbers. Open Loop Scrubbers use seawater to clean exhaust gases, while Closed Loop Scrubbers recirculate water and use chemical additives. Hybrid Scrubbers combine both open and closed loop technologies. Dry Scrubbers use dry sorbent to remove pollutants, and Membrane Scrubbers separate gases using specialized membranes. These different types cater to diverse ship-sizes, operational requirements, and environmental regulations in the maritime industry.</p></p>
