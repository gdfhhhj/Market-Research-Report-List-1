<p><p>The Antistatic Vacuum Cleaner market can be divided into three types: Single Phase Type, Three-Phase Type, and Pneumatic Type. Single Phase Type vacuum cleaners are commonly used in households and small spaces, operating on a single phase electrical power supply. Three-Phase Type vacuum cleaners are mainly used in industrial settings, as they require a higher electrical power supply and can handle heavier debris. Pneumatic Type vacuum cleaners, on the other hand, operate using compressed air and are commonly used in environments where electricity is not feasible or safe.</p></p>
