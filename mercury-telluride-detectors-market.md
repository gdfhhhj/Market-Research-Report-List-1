<p><p>Mercury Telluride Detectors are used in various applications such as infrared spectroscopy, environmental monitoring, and industrial process control. These detectors can be categorized into two types: photovoltaic and photoconductive. Photovoltaic detectors are based on the photovoltaic effect, where incoming radiation generates a voltage directly. This type of detector is used in applications that require high speed and low noise operation. On the other hand, photoconductive detectors rely on changes in conductivity under the influence of radiation, and they are commonly used in applications where high sensitivity and wide spectral range are required.</p></p>
