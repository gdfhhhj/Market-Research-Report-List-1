<p><p>Device circuit breakers are electrical switches designed to protect electrical devices from damage due to overcurrent. The market for device circuit breakers is categorized into three types: plug-in type, stationary type, and draw-out type. </p><p>Plug-in type circuit breakers are easy to install and remove as they simply plug into the electrical panel. Stationary type circuit breakers, on the other hand, are permanently fixed in the panel and cannot be easily removed. Lastly, draw-out type circuit breakers can be partially or fully withdrawn from the panel for maintenance or replacement without interrupting the power supply.</p></p>
