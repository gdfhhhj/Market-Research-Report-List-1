<p><strong>The 3D Electro-optical Image Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stereo Vision</li><li>Structured Light</li><li>Time-of-Flight</li><li>Ultrasound</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3D electro-optical image sensor market is divided into different types: stereo vision, structured light, time-of-flight, and ultrasound. Stereo vision utilizes two cameras to capture images from slightly different perspectives, enabling depth perception. Structured light projects a pattern onto an object and analyzes the deformation, allowing for accurate depth mapping. Time-of-flight measures the time it takes for light to bounce back from an object, providing precise depth information. Ultrasound uses sound waves to detect distances and create a 3D image. These technologies enhance image sensing capabilities in various applications.</p></p>
