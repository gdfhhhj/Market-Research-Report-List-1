<p><p>The Standard Duplex Receptacle Testers market includes two main types: GFCI (Ground Fault Circuit Interrupter) Receptacle Testers and AFCI (Arc Fault Circuit Interrupter) Receptacle Testers. GFCI Receptacle Testers are designed to ensure the proper functioning of Ground Fault Circuit Interrupters, which protect against electrical shocks caused by faulty wiring or appliances. AFCI Receptacle Testers, on the other hand, are used to test Arc Fault Circuit Interrupters, which are designed to prevent electrical fires caused by arc faults in electrical circuits. Both types of testers play a crucial role in electrical safety by detecting and preventing dangerous electrical conditions.</p></p>
