<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) Copolymer is a type of plastic used in various applications. Isotactic Polypropylene refers to the crystalline form of PP, which possesses high tensile strength and stability. Atactic Polypropylene lacks a regular structure and is more flexible and amorphous. Syndiotactic Polypropylene is a combination of both isotactic and atactic forms, resulting in enhanced properties such as heat resistance and improved processability. Each market type has distinct characteristics, making them suitable for different purposes in industries such as automotive, packaging, and textiles.</p></p>
