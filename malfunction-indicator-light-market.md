<p><p>The Malfunction Indicator Light (MIL) market can be divided into two types: Intermittent MIL and Continuous MIL. Intermittent MIL refers to a situation where the light only turns on occasionally, indicating sporadic issues with the vehicle's system. On the other hand, Continuous MIL indicates a persistent problem that requires immediate attention. Both types signify potential malfunctions in the vehicle's engine or emission control system, prompting drivers to seek professional help for repairs. The distinction between the two lies in the consistency of the indicator light.</p></p>
