Titanium alloys offer a combination of high strength and lightness, making them ideal for critical aircraft parts. Super alloys possess excellent heat resistance, making them suitable for engines and exhaust systems. Composites are made up of two or more different materials and are used to reduce weight while maintaining strength and performance in various aircraft components.</p></p>
