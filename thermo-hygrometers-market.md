Digital Type: These thermo-hygrometers feature a digital display that shows precise temperature and humidity measurements. They usually offer more advanced functionalities, such as data logging, trend analysis, and sometimes even wireless connectivity for remote monitoring.</p><p>Overall, both types serve the purpose of measuring temperature and humidity levels, with pointer types being simpler and more traditional, while digital types offer enhanced features and precision.</p></p>
