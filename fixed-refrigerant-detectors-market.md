<p><p>Fixed refrigerant detectors are devices used to detect leaks of refrigerants in various applications such as HVAC systems, refrigeration units, and industrial processes. There are two main types of fixed refrigerant detectors: halide and electronic. Halide detectors use a chemical reaction to detect specific types of refrigerants, such as chlorine or bromine.
