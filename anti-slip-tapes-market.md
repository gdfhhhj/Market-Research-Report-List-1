With an increasing number of people looking for ways to prevent accidents, there is a growing demand for anti-slip tapes in households, particularly in areas prone to wet conditions, such as bathrooms, kitchens, and balconies.</p><p>Overall, the anti-slip tapes market is poised for growth due to the increasing focus on safety, advancements in material technology, and expanding application areas.
