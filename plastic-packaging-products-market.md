<p><p>Plastic packaging products are divided into two main market types: rigid plastic packaging and flexible plastic packaging. Rigid plastic packaging includes containers, bottles, caps, and closures made from stiff and inflexible materials. It is commonly used for products that require proper protection and durability, such as beverages and household cleaning products. On the other hand, flexible plastic packaging refers to materials like bags, films, and wraps that are more pliable and can be easily manipulated. This type of packaging is often used for food products, as it provides convenience and extends shelf life.</p></p>
