<p><p>Polyurea coatings market includes two main types: pure polyurea coating and hybrid polyurea coating. Pure polyurea coatings are made entirely of polyurea resins and are known for their high durability, flexibility, and chemical resistance. They are commonly used for industrial and commercial applications such as automotive, infrastructure, and oil and gas sectors. On the other hand, hybrid polyurea coatings combine polyurea resins with other materials like polyurethane or epoxy resins to enhance certain properties, providing a balance between flexibility and hardness. They find applications in various industries, including construction, flooring, and marine coatings.</p></p>
