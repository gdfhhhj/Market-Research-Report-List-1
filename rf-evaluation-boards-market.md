Manufacturers are focusing on incorporating features like high-frequency range, multiple RF inputs/outputs, and software-defined capabilities to meet the evolving requirements of the industry.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the development of miniaturized RF evaluation boards. With the increasing demand for compact and portable devices, manufacturers are designing smaller evaluation boards without compromising performance. This trend caters to the requirements of applications such as wearables, IoT devices, and connected vehicles.</p><p>Furthermore, there is a growing emphasis on the development of RF Evaluation Boards with improved power efficiency and low noise levels.
