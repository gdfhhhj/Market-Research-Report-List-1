<p><strong>The Stationary Electric Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Type</li><li>Mobile Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stationary electric compressor market includes two main types: fixed type and mobile type. Fixed type compressors are designed to be permanently installed in a specific location, such as an industrial facility or a manufacturing plant. These compressors are typically larger in size and have a higher capacity to meet the continuous air demand of a stationary operation. On the other hand, mobile type compressors are portable and can be easily moved from one location to another. These compressors are often used in construction sites or for emergency situations where compressed air is required on the go.</p></p>
