<p><p>The Waterproof Sheet Material market is categorized into three types based on their composition. Polyester filler (PY) consists of waterproof sheets made from polyester material. Glass fiber filler (G) refers to sheets composed of glass fibers that enhance their waterproof properties. Glass fiber reinforced polyester filler (PYG) combines both polyester and glass fibers to create sheets that are highly durable and offer excellent waterproofing capabilities. These different types cater to specific requirements and preferences of consumers in the Waterproof Sheet Material market.</p></p>
