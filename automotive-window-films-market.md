<p><p>Automotive window films are categorized into two types: automotive window tint and paint protection film. Automotive window tint refers to the process of adding a thin film to the windows of a vehicle to reduce heat and glare from the sun, while also providing privacy. On the other hand, paint protection film is a transparent film that is applied to a vehicle's exterior to protect the paint from scratches, chips, and other damages that may occur during regular use. Both window tint and paint protection film enhance the appearance and durability of vehicles.</p></p>
