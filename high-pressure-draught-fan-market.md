<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The high-pressure draught fan market is divided into three types: centrifugal, axial flow, and inclined flow. Centrifugal fans use high-speed rotating impellers to create pressure, making them suitable for applications requiring high flow rates and medium pressures. Axial flow fans have blades that force air to move parallel to the shaft, making them suitable for applications with high flow rates and low pressures. Inclined flow fans are a combination of both, providing a balance between flow rate and pressure. These fans are used in various industries for ventilation, cooling, and air circulation purposes.</p></p>
