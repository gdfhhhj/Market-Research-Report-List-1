The resistive sensor market includes smart textiles that change their electrical resistance when subjected to external forces. The capacitive sensor market involves smart textiles that alter their capacitance when influenced by external factors. The piezoresistive sensor market comprises smart textiles that change their electrical properties under mechanical stress. All three of these market types offer innovative solutions for various industries, such as wearable technology, healthcare, sports, and automotive, enabling the development of interactive and responsive textiles.</p></p>
