<p><p>The Medical Intraocular Lens market is categorized into four main types: Monofocal Intraocular Lens, Multifocal Intraocular Lens, Toric Intraocular Lens, and Extended Depth of Field Intraocular Lens. Monofocal lenses provide fixed focus at one distance, while Multifocal lenses offer multiple focal points to improve vision at various distances. Toric lenses are specifically designed to correct astigmatism, while Extended Depth of Field lenses enhance vision at various distances by elongating the depth of focus. These different types cater to specific eye conditions and provide patients with improved visual outcomes following cataract surgery or lens replacement.</p></p>
