In the epoxy resin type, prepregs are formed using epoxy resins as the matrix material, ensuring high strength and stability. The thermoplastic resin type uses thermoplastic resins, allowing for easy shaping and reshaping when heated. Lastly, the polyester resin type utilizes polyester resins, offering excellent chemical resistance and electrical insulation properties. These different types cater to varying application needs in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and construction.</p></p>
