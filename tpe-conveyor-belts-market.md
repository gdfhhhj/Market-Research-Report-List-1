<p><p>TPE Conveyor Belts Market types include TPES, TPU, TPO, and TPEE. TPES are thermoplastic elastomers with excellent abrasion resistance, making them suitable for industrial applications. TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) belts offer high flexibility, elasticity, and chemical resistance, making them ideal for food processing and packaging industries. TPO (thermoplastic olefin) belts provide good heat resistance and are commonly used in automotive and industrial applications. TPEE (thermoplastic polyester elastomer) belts offer high tensile strength, durability, and resistance to chemicals and UV radiation, making them suitable for demanding environments such as mining and construction.</p></p>
