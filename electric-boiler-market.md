<p><p>An electric boiler is a heating device that uses electricity as its power source to generate heat. Electric boilers are compact, easy to install, and have no emissions, making them a popular choice for both residential and commercial use.</p><p>The global electric boiler market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. The increasing adoption of electric boilers as a replacement for traditional heating systems, such as gas boilers, is one of the key factors driving market growth. Electric boilers offer several advantages over conventional boilers, including higher efficiency, lower operating costs, and reduced carbon emissions. The growing awareness about environmental conservation and the need for energy-efficient heating solutions are further fueling the market demand for electric boilers.</p><p>Additionally, the rising construction activities, especially in developing economies, are also contributing to market growth.
